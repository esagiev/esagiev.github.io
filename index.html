Welcome

I am a PhD student at the Department of Economics of the University of Essex. Before switching to academics, I was a practicing tax accountant, tax consultant, and tax policy advisor to the Government of Kazakhstan, non-government organizations, and the World Bank.

In 2003, I graduated from the Saint Petersburg State University of Economics with Diploma in Economics. In 2016, I received Master of Research in Economics with distinction in the University of Essex.

Research:

In general, game theory with particular interest in algorithmic game theory and communication games on networks.
